3D Printing Projects

In my third year of RIT, I became very interested in 3D printers. In the spring of 2016, the Research and Development director at CSH appointed me to be a 3D Printer admin. My responsibilities include fixing the printers, keeping filament in the machines and helping members print models for projects. At the start of the 2016-2017 school year I "racked" CSH's 3D printers on some old server racks.

I even installed a 1-U barebones server at the top of the rack to allow people to print remotely. I also installed two IP cameras which allow members to moniter their prints when they cannot be present in the research room.

Future plans include building our large print area 3D printer and makeing a spooling system for the Filibot. Both of these items were kindly donated to CSH from our generous alumni during CSH's 40th anniversary weekend in Spring 2016.

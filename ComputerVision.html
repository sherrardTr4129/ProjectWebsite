Haar-based cascade Mobile Testing Application

I have developed an app that can test these Haar-based cascade files on the go. Paste the URL of a plain text XML file into the text box and hit the button! once the link text disappears you can the click on "my_classifier" from the classifier selection menu. This menu is obtained by clicking on the three dots at the bottom of the screen. This app is available on android devices through the Google play store here.    

High Altitude Balloon Horizon Detection

I have created a program for high altitude balloons to use to find their orientation. It takes images from two 90 degree offset cameras and detects the horizon in each image. The angle relative to the vehicle is then computed. If the vehicle is perpendicular to the horizon, theta should be near 90 degrees or Pi/2 radians. This is done by using Canny edge detection and then applying a hough line transform. Here is a good horizon detection, with the angle offset computed in a counter-clockwise manner, printed in degrees.
